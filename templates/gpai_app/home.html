About Us

Our project focuses on monitoring and enhancing cleanliness across post offices in India. Using advanced AI technologies, we analyze CCTV images captured at scheduled intervals to determine cleanliness status. The system detects garbage near dustbins, categorizes it into types (e.g., plastic, glass, paper), and displays the results on an interactive dashboard. Each post office is mapped with geolocation data, enabling real-time status updates via clean and dirty markers with animated icons. Additionally, the project includes features for document management, such as electricity bill processing, to ensure streamlined operations. Our aim is to promote Swachhta (cleanliness) and LiFE (Lifestyle for Environment) practices while leveraging AI to bring transparency and efficiency to monitoring protocols.
